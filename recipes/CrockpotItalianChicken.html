Crockpot Italian Chicken!

Description

In this recipe, you will be able to learn how to cook a delicious Crockpot Italian Chicken! It has rich flavors consisting of 3 primary ingredients, the bonless skinless chicken breasts, Parmesan Cheese, and Italian Dressing.

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Italian Dressing

Directions

  1. Step 1: You must stir the Italian Dressing and Parmesan Cheese together in a bowl very thoroughly.
  2. S tep 2: Place the chicken breast in a crockpot, and pour the dressing mixture that you have just mixed, over the chicken. Cover the crockpot and cook slowly on low until the chicken is tender, no longer pink in the center, and the juices are running clear. This should last for about 8 hours.
